The Robin Editorial Magazine

Named after the cheerful, resilient songbird, **The Robin** is Brainz Edu World's student-run editorial newsletter. It acts as an organic canvas for creative essays, technology logs, school event coverage, and hand-sketched illustrations.

Created entirely by the student council editorial board, each issue captures conceptual discussions and reviews reflecting student life, values, and scientific curiosity.
